A Place Apart

164 Acres of Pristine Wilderness

Last Wild River Resort offers an experience unlike any other in Oklahoma. Glover River — the last free-flowing, undammed river in the state — winds through our 164-acre private property, creating a sanctuary where the only sounds you'll hear are those of nature.

Surrounded by the Ouachita Mountains and located just 16 miles from Broken Bow and 45 minutes from Beavers Bend State Park, this is where seclusion meets luxury. Hike mountain trails, fish for bass and perch, kayak the river, or simply let the world fall away.
