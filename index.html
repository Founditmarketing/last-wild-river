Our Heritage

Oklahoma's Last Undammed, Free-Flowing River.

The Glover River is an ecological masterpiece. Untouched by man-made dams, it carves its way through the Ouachita Mountains exactly as it has for thousands of years. Last Wild River Resort was thoughtfully founded to not only provide uncompromised luxury, but to grant exclusive, entirely private access to this vanishing wilderness. Here, you aren't just standing in a forest—you are stepping into a living sanctuary.

A Place Apart

164 Acres of Pristine Wilderness

Last Wild River Resort offers an experience unlike any other in Oklahoma. Glover River — the last free-flowing, undammed river in the state — winds through our 164-acre private property. Surrounded by the Ouachita Mountains, this is where seclusion meets luxury.
